What is the Cupcake-inator?

If you are a sweettooth you sure like cupcakes. But did you know that you can now predict your future with the cupcake-inator. The cupcake-inator is a clever machine that analyzes your ingredients to make a prediction. But watch out how unhealthy you make your cupcake this could have devastating effects.
